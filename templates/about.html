Agriculture industry

Agriculture industry is one of the vital and important industry for any country as it produces different products which are the major source of energy for all the individuals and these products helps us to maintain the flow of energy.

But since the last few decades this industry is in a critical condition because of the problems created by the humans itself, problems like climate change, global warming, soil pollution. have been drastically reducing the production of this industry.

Also, the steps taken to increase the production of this industry (for example usage of pesticides, fertilizers) are becoming a threat for us only, causing many serious diseases.
